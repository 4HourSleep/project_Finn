Description:
This project is a browser-based visual novel game titled "Klesinister Y: Visual Novel." The game follows the character Klesinister Y, a being with a complex background and personality, as they navigate through a virtual mental asylum. Players make choices that influence the narrative's direction, leading to multiple endings. The game is developed using p5.js, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, providing an interactive storytelling experience.

How to Use the Program:
- Launch the game in a web browser.
- Read the narrative displayed on the screen.
- When presented with choices, click on the buttons to select an option.
- Your choices will affect the progression and outcome of the story.
- Continue making choices to explore different narrative paths and endings.
